About Me

I am a first year student studying Music Media & Performance Technology (MMPT) in UL. I chose this course because I have always had an interest in music and recording/producing. I have studied music from a very early age, achieving as high as Grade 5 in the ABRSM Music Academy exams, as well as learning to play and now teaching the guitar and ukulele.

In 2020, I competed in Feile Luimnigh and won gold in the popular song category, and I have also performed many times onstage for audiences, making money from busking and gigging in Ireland and in Spain. I have also involved myself in acting and musical theatre, playing the lead role in a production of the musical 'We Will Rock You', as well as playing an extra in the upcoming movie 'One Night Only', directed by David Gleeson.
